ABOUT/the.artist

HELLO WORLD - I'm Feravyre.

I'm a multidisciplinary artist and illustrator with a focus on creature design and worldbuilding. Creating emotional, immersive experiences is my goal, and loneliness, fragility, and the humanity of inhuman things are themes I like to explore in my work.

While I'm proficient in Photoshop and Illustrator, my personal illustration and design work is produced in Clip Studio Paint and Affinity Designer while my animated pixel work is mostly done in Aseprite.

Beyond the world of art, I've earned an Associate's in web design and along the way I've developed my skills with Java, C++, and Javascript. Despite all the time I spend plugged in, my off time is more often spent wandering the woods near my home or caring for my doves.

If you're planning an upcoming project and you're interested in working with me, I hope we can connect; I always look forward to bringing fresh ideas to life.

Warmly, respectfully,
