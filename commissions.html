Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you (the client) agree to the following conditions:

  • Payment is accepted via Paypal and prices are listed in USD. All paypal or other transactional fees will be covered by me, the artist. Clients are never responsible for paying surcharges to cover these fees.
  • Clients may not claim commissioned artwork as their own creation. Please give proper credit for completed work.
  • If I cannot complete a commission for any reason, the client will be promptly contacted via their preferred method of contact and fully refunded.
  • If I am awaiting a response/approval from a client for longer than two weeks, their commission will be canceled and refunded.
  • Commissioned artwork will be posted to my social media and/or portfolio(s) at my discretion unless otherwise is specifically requested by the client.
