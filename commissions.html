Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you agree to the following conditions:

  • Payment is accepted via Paypal and prices are listed in USD. All paypal or other transactional fees will be covered by me, the artist. Buyers are never responsible for paying surcharges to cover these fees.
  • Finished artwork will be completed and delivered via the original point of contact within one month of payment being received.
  • Commissioners may reupload or repost their completed commissions only if proper credit is given; commissioners may not claim commissioned artwork as their own creation.
  • If I cannot complete a commission for any reason, the commissioner will be promptly contacted via their preferred method of contact and fully refunded.
  • If I am awaiting a response/approval from a commissioner for longer than two weeks, their commission will be canceled and refunded.
  • Commissioned artwork may be posted to my social media and portfolio(s) at my discretion unless otherwise is specifically requested by the commissioner.
