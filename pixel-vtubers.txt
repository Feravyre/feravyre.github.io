<h3>Rigging</h3>
<p>
    There are two rigging styles I use - I call them "smooth" and "snap." Smooth rigging is what you typically see in 2D VTubers, with only a few elements being hand-drawn to mesh with the pixel art style. Snap rigging requires many frames to be hand-drawn, making it pricier, but gives the satisfying appearance of real pixel animation. <br>
    See more examples of my rigging work <a href="">here</a>.
</p>
